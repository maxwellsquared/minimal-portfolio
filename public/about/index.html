I'm originally from the Bay Area, but I moved to Vancouver to go to UBC where I studied English, art, and film while volunteering for CiTR 101.9FM campus/community radio. Now I live with my partner and our dog in Victoria, which is the traditional, unceded territory of the Lekwungen (Esquimalt and Songhees) people.

After a decade of working in marketing and communications, I realised that writing code was the part of my work I loved the most, so I made the slightly terrifying decision to quit my awesome job and pursue programming full-time.

Some things that are important to me:

  • Accessibility - I want everybody to be able to enjoy the things I've made!
  • Design - I like beautiful things that are easy to use!
  • Collaboration - I love working with all kinds of people to make awesome stuff!
  • Community - I've received so much support from my local community and I love giving back!

Some of my interests and hobbies include:

  • VR/AR
  • AI/machine learning
  • Modelling and rendering in Blender 3D
  • Graphic design
  • 3D printing
  • Making music with synthesizers, laptops, and traditional instruments
  • Deejaying (I used to be a full-time nightclub DJ!)
  • Campus/community radio
  • Drag (especially local performers!)
  • Painting little plastic elves
